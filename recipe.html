Beet Root Powder

Beet Root Powder can be used for a varitey of recipes. Wether they be for coctails, gym drinks, pastry coloring, and meat seasoning

Here's a super easy recipe for turning your Beet Roots into a powder!

Step by Step Beet Root Powder creation

  • 3 Bunches of Beets (4 or 5 Beets in each bunch)
  • Air Fryer, Oven, or Dehydrater
  • Jar to store powder

  1. Gather 4 Beet roots and wash them with vingar.
  2. Once washed, peel the skin of youe beet roots with a peeler or a knife.
  3. Thinly slice your skinless beet roots.
  4. Set your oven to 150 degrees Fereheit.
  5. Using a cooking sheet or Aluminum foil, spread your thingly sliced Beet Roots onto the sheet or Aluminum Foil. Make sure they are spread out evenly.
  6. Set a timer for 2.5 Hours, and peridocically check on your beets.
  7. Once cooled you can place them in a blender, coffee grinder, or Ninja Bullet. This step with transform your dried beet roots into a powder.
  8. You can store your powder in a dark and cool place.
